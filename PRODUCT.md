# Product

## Name
JGDIFF (available at https://jgdiff.gg)

## Description
JGDIFF is a site that specifically aims to help Junglers, arguably the most impactful role in League of Legends, perform at a higher level. From detailed in-depth player analysis to match review insights to pregame scouting on teammates and opponents, we are determined to give Junglers all the information they need to achieve a JG Diff in their next game!

_Important Note: This site is most useful to junglers but non-junglers can still greatly benefit from it!_

## Unique Features
- Scouting Report map that helps a jungler better strategize which lanes and monster objectives to prioritize
- Analysis feature that breaks down a player's strengths and weaknesses in all roles, but especially the Jungle role
- Performance timeline that helps you track your progress over time and identify trends in your playstyle
