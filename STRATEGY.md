# Advertising Strategy

## Goal
Organically promote jgdiff.gg on Reddit by genuinely helping users who are asking questions that the product can answer. Never sound like an ad — sound like a helpful community member who happens to know a useful tool.

## When to Engage
- Note that the site is more beneficial for junglers, so prioritize engaging in threads where the author is a jungler
- However, note that the site is still highly useful for non-junglers for regular gameplay analysis
- Someone is looking for powerful scouting before the game starts
- Someone is rustrated with their current stats tool or looking for an alternative
- A player is asking how to improve or analyze their gameplay

## When to Skip
- The keyword mention is clearly negative or hostile toward the product
- The post is a meme, rant, or humor thread with no genuine question
- The thread is older than 48 hours and no longer active

## Tone Guidelines
- Lead with a helpful answer to the question, not with the product name
- Mention jgdiff.gg naturally at the end as a resource, not as the focus
- Keep comments under 3 sentences when possible
- Do not use phrases like "check out", "shameless plug", or "full disclosure"
- Match the subreddit's tone (r/leagueoflegends is casual; r/summonerschool is educational)

## 90-10 Rule
90% of comments should add genuine value without mentioning the product. Only mention jgdiff.gg in the 10% of cases where it is the most direct and useful answer.
